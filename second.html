How To play

The bird flaps upward each time you mouseclick; If the bird touches the pipes, you lose.
if the mouse is not clicked, the bird falls because of gravity.
You earn a point when you sucessfully navigate a pair of pipes.
There is no variation or evolution in gameplay throughout the game,
as the pipes always have the same gap between them and there is no end to the running track,
having only the flap sounds.
